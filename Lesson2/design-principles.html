Hick's law

My own!

jenifierlynn.github.io

Hick's law is simple, "keep it simple". Today there are so many options when it comes to shoppng or browsing the internet. If you can find a website with fewer choices, then the user can simply click on one area to get to where they are going instead of wasting time trying to figure out which part of which link they wish to visit.
